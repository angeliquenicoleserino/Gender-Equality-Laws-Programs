Key Laws Promoting Gender Equality

RA 9262: Violence Against Women and Children (VAWC)

Objective: To protect women and children from violence in relationships, providing penalties for abusive behavior.

Key Provisions: Includes psychological violence, physical abuse, and economic control, among others.

Penalties: Imprisonment and fines based on the severity of the crime.

Why it’s Important: It ensures that women and children can live free from violence in all forms.

Case Scenario

Incident: A woman is being stalked and harassed by her former partner. Despite multiple warnings, he continues to make threats and intrudes on her personal space. The woman reports the case under the Anti-Sexual Harassment Law (RA 7877).

How the Law Protects the Victim: The law provides mechanisms to file complaints, investigation, and if proven, the abuser is penalized with fines and/or imprisonment. This protects the woman from further harm and provides legal protection for her safety.
